WELCOME

DEYA Brewing Company is an independent brewery in Cheltenham, UK. We focus on hoppy beers, lagers,
traditional UK styles and mixed fermentation ales. We strive to make world class beer.

Our on-site Taproom is at the heart of our brewery and is open every week for fresh tins and delicious draft
pours.

We take a holistic approach to business, striving to be the best employer we possibly can be, while
operating in a fair, sustainable and human driven way.
